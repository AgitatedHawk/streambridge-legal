# StreamBridge Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Username and display name
- Password (encrypted)

### Platform Connections
- OAuth tokens for TikTok, YouTube, Twitch, Kick
- Platform usernames and user IDs
- Access tokens (encrypted and stored securely)

### Usage Data
- Stream events and earnings
- Game activations
- Platform connection status

### Automatically Collected
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Login timestamps

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide the StreamBridge service
- Process earnings and payouts
- Connect to streaming platforms via OAuth
- Send notifications about your account
- Improve our service
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## 3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We share information only:
- With streaming platforms (via OAuth) to enable the service
- With payment processors for payouts
- When required by law
- With your explicit consent

## 4. Data Security

We protect your data using:
- Encryption for passwords and sensitive tokens
- Secure HTTPS connections
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

## 5. OAuth and Third-Party Platforms

### TikTok
- We access: Basic user info, video list
- Purpose: Track gifts and donations
- You can revoke access anytime via TikTok settings

### YouTube
- We access: Channel info, live stream data
- Purpose: Track Super Chats and memberships
- You can revoke access via Google Account settings

### Twitch
- We access: User info, subscription data
- Purpose: Track bits, subs, and donations
- You can revoke access via Twitch connections settings

### Kick
- Currently simulated (no official API)

## 6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:
- Authentication and session management
- Remembering your preferences

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of marketing communications

## 8. Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data deleted within 30 days
- Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

## 9. Children's Privacy

StreamBridge is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 10. International Users

Your data may be transferred to and stored in the United States.

## 11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.

## 12. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights:
- Email: privacy@streambridge.io
- Subject: Privacy Request
